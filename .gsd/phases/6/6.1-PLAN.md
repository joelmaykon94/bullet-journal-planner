# Plan 6.1: Trash Bin (Lixeira) & Someday/Maybe (Algum Dia) Tab

## Objective
Implement a Recycle Bin (Lixeira) to store deleted items with restore capabilities, and a Someday/Maybe (Algum Dia) GTD-compliant workspace for tracking unscheduled tasks and ideas.

## Context
- .gsd/SPEC.md
- src/context/BujoContext.tsx
- src/hooks/useBujoItems.ts
- src/features/planner/components/Sidebar.tsx
- src/App.tsx

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Deleted BujoItems go to the Trash Bin instead of being permanently removed.
- [ ] Users can restore items from the Trash Bin back to their original date log.
- [ ] Users can create and manage Someday/Maybe ideas, and schedule them into their daily bullet journal logs.
- [ ] Application builds successfully without type or compilation errors.
