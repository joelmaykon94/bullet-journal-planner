# Plan 6.2: Task Delegation & Icon/Avatar Selection

## Objective
Enable users to delegate tasks to others (adding a visual person badge) and customize list items, tasks, or subtasks with drawing-like emojis/icons for improved cognitive clarity (ADHD-friendly visual cues).

- src/hooks/useBujoItems.ts
- src/context/BujoContext.tsx
- src/features/planner/components/BulletItem.tsx
