# Plan 9.3: Weekly & Monthly Log Day Modals

## Objective
Implement interactive modals in the Weekly Log and Monthly Log tabs to manage tasks:
1. Create a reusable `DayTasksModal` component that allows viewing, editing, deleting, splitting (IA), and completing tasks, plus adding subtasks and quick-adding new items for a specific date.
2. Integrate `DayTasksModal` in `WeeklyLogTab` so clicking any week day opens it.
3. Integrate `DayTasksModal` in `MonthlyLogTab` so clicking any calendar day opens it.

## Context
- src/features/planner/components/WeeklyLogTab.tsx
- src/features/planner/components/MonthlyLogTab.tsx
